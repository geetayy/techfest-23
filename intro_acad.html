History

Introduction

Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering & Technology (SLIET) Longowal has received deemed University status on April 10, 2007 to run various Integrated Certificate and Diploma (ICD), Under Graduate (UG), Post Graduate (PG) and Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph. D.) programmes under an autonomous academic pattern. The Institute follows a semester system (i.e., two semesters in an academic year). Each semester spans eighteen weeks (i.e., 90 days), excluding end-of-semester examinations (ESE). The academic study schemes at each level are balanced with a number of co-curricular activities and the AICTE pattern. To motivate and encourage the students, a counselling system is also in place which involves various faculty members who act as class counsellors under the guidance of the chief counselor. Students are evaluated on a continuous basis through minor examinations, quizzes, assignments, class activities, and end-of-term examinations throughout the semester The medium of instruction is English for all the courses run by the Institute. The academic section has three distinct cells to deal with the academic matters related to various programmes separately. ICD Cell: The cell deals with matters related to certificate and diploma courses. UG Cell: The cell deals with the undergraduate (UG) courses. PG Cell: The cell Post graduate (PG) and Ph.D. programmes
