We are currently accepting applications for the following programs:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Chemical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Food Technology
  • B.Tech in Instrumentation and Control Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electrical Engineering

To apply, please follow the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of SLIET
  • Click on the "Admissions" tab and select your desired program
  • Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details
  • Upload the required documents (scanned copies of your passport-size photograph, signature, and academic transcripts)
  • Pay the application fee online via net banking, credit/debit card or other payment options available
  • Submit the application form and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference

If you have any queries related to the admissions process, please contact us at admissions@sliet.ac.in
